ABOUT

Tech Witches is a platform for popular education and community organization within the fields of digital arts and tech. The group specifically focuses on issues that are specific to women, gender non-confirming, trans, and marginalized folks. We aim to create safe and accessible spaces for people to learn, discuss and create projects. More broadly, the aim of the group is to introduce marginalized people to the tech world, which can often be very hostile to them at times. Themes which Tech Witches organizes around include: Cybersecurity for women, BIPOCS, activists, artists, and sex workers Tech literacy Game-making and experimental games Radical digital spaces Social media as fine art
